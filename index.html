About P.E.A

Pal's Eat Afrique LLC is a Japan-based trading house built on one idea: when Africa and Japan trade directly, both sides win.

Our story

A bridge between two continents

P.E.A was founded to close the gap between two markets that have so much to offer each other — Japan's reputation for quality vehicles, electronics, and manufacturing, and Africa's rich food culture, traditional fashion, and growing entrepreneurial energy.

We operate as a full-service import and export partner: sourcing, quality checks, shipping, and after-sale support, so our clients can focus on growing their business rather than managing logistics.
